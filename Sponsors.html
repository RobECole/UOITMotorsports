Why you should sponsor us:

Investment in Future Engineers

UOIT Motorsports provides students of different academic disciplines with a practical hands-on experience incorporating multiple aspects of engineering, manufacturing and business management. Your involvement will enable students to gain invaluable learning opportunities in a real-world environment. Forming a partnership with UOIT motorsports will create a lasting bond leading to future potential business relationships.

Showcasing and Testing Your Product

UOIT Motorsports offers a unique test bed for both existing and future products. The ability to demonstrate the success of a product or aid in the development of a new one can provide learning opportunities for the students while generating results and feedback

Exposure

UOIT Motorsports provides a venue for valuable product and brand advertisement. A partnership with UOIT Motorsports enables your company to be showcased on several different mediums such as clothing, print and web. National competitions offer in-depth media coverage from Speed Channel, CNN, The Discovery Channel, ESPN and local news channels along with print publications such as Racecar Engineering, Car and Driver, Road &Track and SAE Momentum. UOIT Motorsports also takes part in events such as the Canadian International Auto Show, Canadian Motorsports Expo and exhibition events for UOIT and other sponsors. Web IP, viral videos and grassroots advertising are also utilized to gain further exposure both locally and internationally.
